Geo-Trivia App

About the Project

Geo-Trivia is an engaging trivia app designed to test users' knowledge of geography. The app features a rich and interactive user interface combined with a powerful backend to provide a seamless experience. It challenges players with questions related to countries, landmarks, and world capitals, offering an educational and entertaining experience. The app also includes multiple difficulty levels and dynamic question generation to keep users engaged.

Learning Experience

During the development of Geo-Trivia, our team of five collaborated to build a fully functional app. This project provided valuable learning opportunities, including:

  • Understanding and implementing core web development technologies such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
  • Developing backend functionalities using Node.js and integrating MongoDB as the database.
  • Designing and iterating wireframes to ensure a user-friendly interface.
  • Improving teamwork and communication skills through regular meetings, brainstorming sessions, and task delegation.

This experience not only enhanced our technical skills but also taught us the importance of collaboration, version control, and iterative development in creating a successful project.
